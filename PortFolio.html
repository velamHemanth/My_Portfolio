Evaluation of Self-Healing in SDN Topologies with Voice-Driven Control
|Ubuntu,SDN

I conducted an in-depth research project titled "Evaluation of Self-Healing Characteristics of Different Topologies in an SDN Architecture Aided with Voice-Driven Control."
